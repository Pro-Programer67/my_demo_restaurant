Our Story

Honest food,
uncompromising
craft.

Maison Ardent was born from a single conviction: that the oldest cooking method — fire — is still the most revelatory. Every dish begins at the hearth, where smoke, heat, and time transform the finest local ingredients into something extraordinary.

Our suppliers are farmers, fishers, and foragers within 80 miles of our kitchen. The menu changes when the land does. Nothing more, nothing less.

The Kitchen

Chef Lucien
Marais

Executive Chef & Founder

"Fire tells you the truth about an ingredient. Everything else is distraction."

Lucien trained under Heston Blumenthal at The Fat Duck before spending three formative years in the Basque Country, learning live-fire technique from the grill masters of Asador Etxebarri. He returned to open Maison Ardent with a single hearth, twelve tables, and an unshakeable conviction about how food should taste.


Today, Lucien leads a kitchen of seven, sourcing directly from the farms he grew up near, cooking meals he'd want to eat, and refusing to compromise on ingredients — ever.
